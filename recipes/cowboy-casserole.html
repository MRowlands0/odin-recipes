Cowboy Casserole

Description

This hearty cowboy casserole is comfort food at it's best with a creamy filling and crispy tots!

Ingredients

  • 2 slices thick-cut bacon, chopped
  • 1 cup chopped yellow onion
  • 1 cup chopped red bell pepper
  • 1 pound ground sirloin
  • 8 ounces hot pork country sausage (such as Jimmy Dean)
  • 2 teaspoons chopped garlic

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C) with a rack in the center position.
  2. Heat a large cast-iron skillet over medium. Add bacon and cook, stirring often, until browned and crisp, about 7 minutes.
  3. Add onion and bell pepper to drippings in skillet; cook, stirring occasionally, until softened, about 4 minutes. Increase heat to medium-high and add ground beef and sausage.
